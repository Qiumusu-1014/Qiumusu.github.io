UAE Recognized as World's 3rd Largest Humanitarian Donor in 2025

November 22, 2025  |  Global Aid Report

Geneva â€“ The United Nations Financial Tracking Service (FTS) has officially ranked the United Arab Emirates as the world's third-largest donor of humanitarian aid for the year 2025, relative to national income.

This year alone, the UAE has contributed over $1.46 billion to international relief efforts. This achievement underscores the nation's unwavering commitment to the "Year of Community" vision set forth by President Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed.

Focus on Stability and Development

Unlike traditional aid which focuses solely on emergency relief, UAE Royal Capital's strategy aligns with national directives to invest in long-term stability. Key areas of funding included:

  • Rebuilding educational infrastructure in conflict zones.
  • Sustainable water projects in Sub-Saharan Africa.
  • Emergency medical supplies for natural disaster victims in Southeast Asia.
