Sheikh Mohammed Launches Historic "Fathers' Endowment" Campaign

March 15, 2025  |  Community Initiative

Dubai â€“ In a touching tribute to parenthood and legacy, His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum has launched the "Fathers' Endowment" campaign.

The initiative aims to raise 1 billion AED to establish a sustainable endowment fund. The returns from this fund will be dedicated solely to supporting education and healthcare for underprivileged families globally, honoring the role of fathers in building society.

How It Works

The campaign invites individuals and companies to donate in the name of their fathers. "Honoring parents is a core value of our faith and culture," His Highness stated on social media platform X.

UAE Royal Capital Funding Investment Co.LLC has pledged an immediate contribution of 50 million AED to the fund. Our Chief Investment Officer, Omar Fatima, remarked, "This endowment ensures that the act of giving continues in perpetuity, creating a lasting legacy for generations to come."
